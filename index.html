About

Hi I’m Pareshma, I’m all about 80’s music and making the world a better place. I’m a freelance multimedia designer and developer based in Toronto, Ontario who specializes in motion, graphic, and web development.

I graduated from the Web Design & Interactive Media program at Humber College in 2020. During my years of study, I highly focused on combining user experience with principles of design. I strive to create great user experiences in my field of expertise with consistency, usability, and good design. I’m proficient in HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, and the Adobe Creative Cloud Suite, allowing me to help you with whatever you need.

I’m a passionate creative who loves working with people and I’m always up to trying new things. Feel free to contact me if there’s a project you would like to work on.
